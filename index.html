❌ Critical Issues

❌ No FAQ or Q&A Content

Why this hurts AI visibility: AI systems (especially Google AI Overviews and Perplexity) preferentially cite pages that directly answer questions in a Q&A format. Without FAQ content, the site has no "answer-ready" text for AI to extract and quote when someone asks "How much does it cost to learn Serbian online?" or "What is the best Serbian language school?"

Fix

Add a FAQ section to the homepage and key service pages. Example:

Q: How much do Serbian online lessons cost?
A: Belgrade Language School offers private lessons starting from €X/hour, group courses from €X, and self-study courses from €X.

Q: How long does it take to learn Serbian?
A: Most students reach conversational Serbian in 3–6 months with regular lessons.

Q: Do I need any prior knowledge of Serbian to start?
A: No. We offer lessons for complete beginners through advanced speakers.

Also add FAQPage JSON-LD schema:

{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [{
    "@type": "Question",
    "name": "How much do Serbian lessons cost?",
    "acceptedAnswer": {
      "@type": "Answer",
      "text": "Our private lessons start from €X/hour..."
    }
  }]
}

❌ Open Graph Tags Not Confirmed Present

Why this hurts AI visibility: Open Graph tags control how your site appears when shared on social media and are also read by AI crawlers to understand page identity. Without them, link previews are broken and AI tools get a weaker signal about what the page is.

Fix
<meta property="og:type" content="website" />
<meta property="og:title" content="Belgrade Language School | Learn Serbian Online" />
<meta property="og:description" content="The world's leading online Serbian language school. Private lessons, group courses, and self-study programs with university-educated native teachers." />
<meta property="og:image" content="https://belgradelanguageschool.com/wp-content/uploads/Serbian-Teacher.jpg" />
<meta property="og:url" content="https://belgradelanguageschool.com/" />
<meta property="og:site_name" content="Belgrade Language School" />

❌ Zero External Authority Signals

Why this hurts AI visibility: AI systems build confidence in a source by how often it appears in credible external contexts — press coverage, directory listings, academic references, travel blogs, expat forums. A site that exists only in isolation is less likely to be cited.

Quick Wins

1. Submit to language school directories: Languagebookings.com, Verbling, Italki, Preply
2. Create a Google Business Profile (even as an online business)
3. Get listed in Wikidata as a language education organization
4. Pitch a feature to expat blogs covering Serbia
5. Add sameAs to your JSON-LD:

  "https://www.facebook.com/BelgradeLanguageSchool/",
